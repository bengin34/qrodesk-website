Privacy Policy

Last updated: Feb. 12, 2026

Deep Work Challenge Tracker is designed as a local-first app and does not require an account.

Data we collect

None. We do not collect, transmit, or store user data on our servers.

Data stored on your device

  • Daily records: date, completion state, minutes, optional distraction tag, freeze usage.
  • User plan data: Pro status and freeze balance.
  • Routine settings: selected time and reminder enabled state.

Where data is stored

Your data is stored on your device in local app storage. Core functionality works without a backend server.

Notifications

If you enable routine reminders, the app uses local notifications to remind you at your selected time.

Analytics and tracking

The app does not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics.

Backups and sync

Optional cloud backup may be added in a future version (for example iCloud or Google backup). If enabled, you will be informed in advance and this policy will be updated.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@qrodesk.com.
