Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This privacy page currently covers the WorkEnglish marketing website and pre-launch support contact. Product-specific app privacy details should be updated before public release if the app data model changes.

Website data

The WorkEnglish website is a static website. It does not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics scripts on this page.

Support and early-access contact

If you email us, we receive the information you include in your message, such as your email address and support details. We use that information only to respond to your request and manage pre-launch communication.

Planned app data categories

  • Profession selection and content access preferences
  • Learning progress, review state, and saved practice activity
  • Subscription or purchase status, if applicable

App privacy status

WorkEnglish is in a pre-launch stage. If app-side storage, analytics, or account behavior changes before release, this page should be updated to reflect the final implementation.
