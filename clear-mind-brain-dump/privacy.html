Privacy Policy

Last updated: Feb. 4, 2026

clearMind is designed to keep your brain dumps private. The app works offline-first and does not require an account.

What data is stored

  • Your dumps and local settings (timer, prompts, preferences).
  • A local purchase flag if you unlock paid features.

Where your data lives

Your data stays on your device. We do not store your dumps on our servers.

Exporting your data

You can export your dumps if you enable the paid Save/Export feature.

No tracking by default

clearMind does not include analytics or advertising trackers by default.

Crash reports (optional)

We do not collect crash reports.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at support@qrodesk.com.
