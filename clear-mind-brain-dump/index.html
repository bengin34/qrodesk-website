FAQ

Do I need an account?

No. clearMind runs offline and does not require login.

What happens when the timer ends?

The timer ends quietly. Writing continues and Release appears.

Can I save my dumps?

Saving is a paid feature. Free users can always Release & Clear.

Is there a history or feed?

There is no feed. A read-only history is optional for paid users.
