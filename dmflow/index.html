FAQ

Does DMFlow connect to Instagram?

No. DMFlow does not connect to Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube.

Can DMFlow send messages for me?

No. DMFlow does not read or send messages. You send messages manually in your social app.

How do templates work?

You save your reply text in DMFlow, copy it, and paste it manually.

How much does DMFlow cost?

DMFlow is $5.99 per month. Purchases are handled by Apple. Restore purchases is available in-app.
