Support

Need help with DMFlow? Send us your issue details and we will get back as quickly as possible.

Support email: support@qrodesk.com

Include: device model, iOS version, app version, and short video or screenshot if available.

Quick help

  • Check App Store for the latest app update.
  • Reopen the app and try the same flow again.
  • For subscription issues, use Restore purchases.
  • If issue continues, contact support email above.

Common questions

How do I restore purchases?

Open DMFlow and tap Restore purchases with the same Apple ID used for subscription.

Does DMFlow connect to Instagram or TikTok?

No. DMFlow does not connect to social platforms and does not send or read messages.

Where can I download the app?

Use the App Store link: DMFlow on App Store
