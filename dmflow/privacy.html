Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

DMFlow does not connect to social platforms. It doesn't read or send messages. It's a personal organizer for your conversations.

What DMFlow stores

  • Conversation records you manually enter.
  • Pipeline stage, notes, and reminder dates.
  • Reusable templates you create or edit.
  • Local purchase status for Pro features.

Where your data lives

DMFlow is local-first. Data is stored on your device by default. DMFlow does not pull message content from Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or any social platform.

No messaging automation

DMFlow does not read incoming messages, send outgoing messages, or automate outreach actions. You manually copy templates and manually send replies in your chosen social app.

Payments

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. DMFlow does not store your full payment card details.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@qrodesk.com.
