FAQ

  • CSV export limit — Free users can export 3 files per month. Pro users have unlimited exports.
  • Restore purchase — Tap Settings ▸ Restore Purchase inside the app after reinstalling.
  • Scanning issues — Make sure Camera permission is allowed & use good lighting. Clean your lens.
  • Feature request — Email the idea with subject “Feature Request”. We log and prioritise popular votes.
  • Bug report — Include app version, device model, steps to reproduce, and a screenshot if possible.

Request Data Deletion

Email us at support@qrodesk.com and we’ll erase all locally stored data associated with your device.
