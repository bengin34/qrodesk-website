FAQ

  • Track left and right - Tap the left or right button to start and stop a feeding session.
  • Last feeding time - The app shows how long it has been since the last session on the home screen.
  • Notes - Add short notes to capture anything important for the next feeding.
  • Export report - Use the export option to share a report with a doctor or consultant.
  • Offline use - Everything works without an internet connection.
  • Language - Switch between English, Deutsch, and Turkce in settings.

Delete data

All data is stored locally on your device. To erase it, clear the app data in your device settings or uninstall the app.
