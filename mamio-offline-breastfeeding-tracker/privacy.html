Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Mamio is built to work completely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

Data we collect

None. The app does not send analytics, advertising IDs, or usage data to us or to third parties.

Data stored on your device

  • Feeding sessions, durations, sides, and notes you enter.
  • Daily and weekly summaries generated from your local data.

Exports

If you export a report, the file is created locally on your device and shared by you. We do not receive a copy.

Deleting your data

All data stays on your device. You can remove it by clearing the app data in your device settings or uninstalling the app.
