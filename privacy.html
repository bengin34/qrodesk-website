Privacy Policy

Last updated:

QroDesk does not collect personal data from users. All scans, created codes and history are stored locally on your device and remain under your control.

Data we do not collect

  • No account, name, email or phone number is required to use the app.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs in the initial release.
  • No precise location data.

Purchases

The app may offer a one-time in-app purchase (“Pro”). Purchase and restore are processed by Apple/Google; we only read a non-personal receipt to unlock features.

Support

If you contact us by email, we will receive your email address and message contents. This information is used solely to respond to your request and is deleted upon resolution.

Third-party services

No advertising SDKs are used in the initial release. If this changes, we will update this policy.

Your rights

You can delete the app to remove all locally stored data. For any questions: Support Qrodesk.
