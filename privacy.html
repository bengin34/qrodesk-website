Privacy Policy

Last updated:

QroDesk values your privacy. All scans and generated codes stay on-device; no cloud copy is kept unless you explicitly share or back-up the file.

Data we collect

  • Camera access — used locally to scan QR / barcodes. Frames are not transmitted or stored on our servers.
  • Crash & usage metrics — anonymous crash logs via Sentry (contains device model, OS version, stack-trace).
  • Advertising ID — free-tier users only, for personalised ads if you grant iOS ATT / Android AAID permission.

Data we do not collect

  • No account signup, username, real name, e-mail or phone is required.
  • No precise GPS location.
  • No contacts, calendar or photo library contents.

Purchases

“Pro” is a one-time in-app purchase. Payment is processed by Apple App Store / Google Play. We store only a non-personal receipt hash to unlock features.

Third-party services

• Sentry — crash reporting (anonymous).
• Google AdMob — banner ads for non-Pro users. Ad personalise- ation follows your ATT / Google Play Services consent choices.

Your choices

  • Delete history in-app: Settings ▸ Clear History.
  • Opt-out of personalised ads: Settings ▸ Privacy.
  • Request data deletion: email us (see below).
