Privacy Policy

Last updated:

QroDesk values your privacy. All scans and generated codes stay on-device; no cloud copy is kept unless you explicitly share or back-up the file.

Data we collect

  • Microphone access — used locally to capture audio input for features that require sound. Recordings are not transmitted or stored on our servers.
  • Crash & usage metrics — anonymous crash logs via Sentry (contains device model, OS version, stack-trace).

Data we do not collect

  • No account signup, username, real name, e-mail or phone is required.
  • No precise GPS location.
  • No contacts, calendar or photo library contents.

Purchases

“Pro” is available as either a monthly subscription or a lifetime one-time purchase. Payments are processed by Apple App Store / Google Play. We store only a non-personal receipt hash to unlock features.
