Privacy Policy

Last updated: Jan. 24, 2026]

VoyageLog is designed to keep your travel history private. The app works offline-first and does not require an account.

What data is stored

  • We do not store any data.

Where your data lives

Your data is stored locally on your device by default. We do not copy your data.

Exporting your data

You can export your trips to PDF or CSV for your own backups or sharing.

No tracking by default

VoyageLog does not include analytics or advertising trackers by default.

Crash reports (optional)

We do not collect crash reports.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at support@qrodesk.com.
