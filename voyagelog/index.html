FAQ

Is VoyageLog a photo search app?

No. VoyageLog is a manual travel journal focused on trips, notes, and places.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Your data stays on-device and the core experience works offline.

Do I need an account?

No account is required to use VoyageLog.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export trips to PDF or CSV whenever you need a backup.
