FAQ

  • Sounds stop when screen locks — Disable battery saver and allow background audio in system settings.
  • Timer fade-out — Set a timer and enable “Fade out” to lower volume before stopping, so baby stays asleep.
  • Restore purchase — Tap Settings ▸ Restore Purchase after reinstalling.
  • Offline use — All bundled sounds work offline; download any extra packs before travel.
  • Bug report — Include app version, device model, steps to reproduce, and a screenshot if possible.
