Privacy Policy

Last updated:

SleepBaby is built to work offline. Soothing sounds, timers, and sleep logs stay on-device unless you choose to share or back them up yourself.

Data we collect

  • Crash & usage metrics — anonymous crash logs (device model, OS version, stack trace) to improve stability.
  • Advertising ID — free-tier users only, used for personalised ads if you grant iOS ATT / Android AAID permission.

Data we do not collect

  • No account signup, username, real name, email, or phone number is required.
  • No microphone recordings are uploaded; the app only plays locally stored sounds.
  • No precise GPS location.

Purchases

“Pro” is a one-time in-app purchase. Payment is processed by Apple App Store / Google Play. We store only a non-personal receipt hash to unlock features.

Third-party services

• Sentry — crash reporting (anonymous).
• Google AdMob — banner ads for non-Pro users. Ad personalisation follows your ATT / Google Play consent choices.

Your choices

  • Delete sleep logs in-app: Settings ▸ Clear History.
  • Opt out of personalised ads: Settings ▸ Privacy.
  • Request data deletion: email us (see below).
