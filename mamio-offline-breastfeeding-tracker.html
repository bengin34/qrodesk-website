Mamio

A simple, offline baby feeding tracker designed for breastfeeding moms.

Track feeding sessions with one tap, even at 3:00 AM, using just one hand.

Designed for real nights

Built for real-life breastfeeding moments: sleepy nights, tired hands, and zero mental energy. No complicated setup. No learning curve. Just start and stop when feeding.

What you can do

  • Track breastfeeding sessions with left and right side.
  • Automatically record feeding duration.
  • See how long it has been since the last feeding.
  • Always know which side is next.
  • Add short notes when you want.
  • Review daily and weekly summaries at a glance.

Your data stays with you

  • Works completely offline.
  • No account required.
  • No ads and no trackers.
  • All data is stored locally on your device.

Your baby's data belongs to you - not to servers, not to advertisers.

Understand your routine

  • Review past feeding sessions.
  • See simple summaries by day.
  • Export reports when you need to share them with a doctor or consultant.
