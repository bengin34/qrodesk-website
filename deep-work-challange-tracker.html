Deep Work Challenge Tracker

Complete one deep work session every day. Build consistency in 30 days.

What this product does

  • Tracks daily completion with 30/60/90 minute session presets.
  • Runs a derived streak engine from daily records.
  • Shows a 30-day heatmap and challenge progress.
  • Adds routine reminders with one-tap Done/Skip actions.
  • No account needed. We do not collect user data.
  • Supports freeze and Pro-gated advanced features.

Core principle

Not a distraction blocker. A consistency system: one rule, one daily action, one visible chain.
