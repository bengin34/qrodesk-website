Privacy Policy

Last updated: Feb 11, 2026

Offly is built for private, offline memory keeping. The app keeps your family's data on your device by default. You decide if and when to export a backup file.

What data Offly stores

  • Chapters, memories, milestones, letters, and attachments you add.
  • Local preferences (themes, filters, chapter labels).
  • Local purchase flag if you unlock Offly Pro.

Where your data lives

All data is stored locally on your device. Offly does not send your content to cloud servers or third parties. There are no social or sharing features.

Backups and exports

Offly Pro lets you export your archive as an encrypted local file that you can store on your computer or drive. You control when exports happen and where they are saved. Imports are local as well.

Accounts and login

Offly does not require an account or sign-up to use the app.

Analytics and tracking

Offly does not include analytics SDKs or advertising trackers by default. Crash reports are not collected automatically.

Children's data

Offly is designed for parents and guardians to keep family memories. Please do not post content publicly from Offly; the app is private and offline-only.
