FAQ

Do I need an account?

No. Offly works offline and never requires login.

Is my data ever uploaded?

No cloud sync by default. Everything is stored locally on your device. Export only when you trigger a backup file.

Can I switch devices?

Yes. Use Offly Pro to export a backup file, then import it on a new device. No servers involved.

What are age-locked letters?

Letters you write now and lock until a target age—like 1st birthday, 5 years, or 18 years. They remain sealed until that date.
